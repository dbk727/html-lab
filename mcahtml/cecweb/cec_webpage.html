ABOUT US

The college of Engineering Chengannur (CEC), was established by the Government of Kerala under the aegis of the IHRD,in the year 1993.It has been approved by AICTE and affiliated toAPJ Abdul Kalam Technological University(KTU).Ever since its inception,the college has maintained its eminence district the college has got access to all means of transport,communication and loading facilities.

COURSE OFFERED

Admission to the courses is on an annual basis on the All Kerala common Enterance Examination conducted by The Controller of Entrance examination,Government of Kerala.The proportion of seat are as follows:Free/merit seat 50%,management seat 35%(as aforementioned allotment to both these categories are through Cental allotment process
